Users are permitted to use the GITA software exclusively for monitoring, analysis, and optimization of Kubernetes clusters. The GITA software must not be used for illegal, fraudulent, or harmful purposes.

Users are responsible for ensuring the security of their access credentials to the GITA software. The team responsible for the GITA software reserves the right to carry out updates, scheduled maintenance, and bug fixes as necessary to ensure the quality of service. Users will be notified in advance of any planned service interruptions, whenever possible.

7. Technical Support:

The technical support team is available to assist users with any questions, technical issues, or assistance requests related to the GITA software. Users can contact the support team through channels designated by the company.

Any violation of this policy may result in the suspension or termination of the user's access to the GITA software, at the company's discretion. The company reserves the right to take legal action against users engaging in unauthorized or illegal activities using the GITA software.

GITA Subscription or Service Cancellation Policy

This Subscription or Service Cancellation Policy ('Policy') establishes the procedures and guidelines for canceling subscriptions or services of GITA, a health monitoring and best practices service for Kubernetes clusters ('Service'). Our policy aims to ensure a transparent and convenient experience for our users, allowing them to cancel their subscription or service at any time, without fines or penalties.

Pricing and Billing

The price of the GITA service is determined based on the usage of Kubernetes nodes per hour, for each cluster associated with the user's subscription. Kubernetes nodes are measured and summed for each hour of the monthly billing period. We do not charge a cancellation fee. Users have the right to cancel their subscription at any time, and the cancellation will take effect immediately upon user confirmation.

Monthly Billing

Billing for the GITA service is closed monthly. Users will be billed based on the total number of Kubernetes nodes used throughout the month.

Billing Calculation

Billing will be calculated by multiplying the total number of Kubernetes nodes by the price per node, as established at the time of subscription. The price per node may vary based on factors such as additional features offered or volume discounts - but always with mutual acceptance and alignment.

Subscription Cancellation

Users have the right to stop using the service at any time, with no additional charges. Upon subscription cancellation, access to the GITA service will be maintained until the end of the current billing period. There will be no refund of fees already paid.

Billing and Payment

Billing will be processed automatically based on usage data collected by the GITA system. Users must provide valid and up-to-date payment information to ensure efficient billing processing. Payment is due within the timeframe established at the time of billing. Non-payment may result in suspension or termination of the user's subscription.

Changes to Pricing and Billing Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Pricing and Billing Policy at any time, with prior notice to users. Changes to pricing or billing practices will be communicated to users in advance, and the changes will not take effect until the next billing cycle. This Pricing and Billing Policy is subject to the GITA Terms of Service. In case of conflict between this Policy and the Terms of Service, the Terms of Service shall prevail. If you have any questions or need further assistance regarding pricing and billing, please contact our customer support team. We are here to help.
